# Institutional Governance Patterns

Use this reference when drafting, reviewing, or designing documents and workflows involving党委、人大、政府、政协, 三定方案, 机构编制,会议流程议程, or公务/政务接待.

## Skill Feature Summary

This skill is a procedural government-office assistant, not only a writing template. It combines:

- Document-type judgment: choose the right文种, recipient relationship, authority, and issuing path.
- Format control: use official document elements, GB/T 9704-style layout expectations, fonts, line grid, page numbers, and printable QA.
- Workflow awareness: route drafts through拟稿、审核、会签、合法性/合规审查、签发、印发、督办、归档.
- Executable plans: convert slogans into lead unit, support unit, deliverable, timeline, reporting path, budget, and closure standard.
- Institutional boundaries: keep党委、人大、政府、政协, departments,事业单位,议事协调机构, and territorial governments in their proper roles.
- Evidence discipline: require source documents, local templates, latest三定规定, meeting rules, budget basis, and approval records before asserting authority.

## Four-Body Role Boundaries

When a task crosses multiple organs, first identify which body has decision authority, execution authority, consultation/supervision role, and document-issuing authority.

### 党委

Use党委 language for leadership, overall coordination, major policy direction, cadre/organization matters, party-building, security and stability leadership, ideology, discipline, and cross-system coordination.

Typical bodies and documents:

- 党委常委会, 党委全会, 专题会议, 领导小组会议, 议事协调机构会议.
- 决定, 意见, 通知, 会议纪要, 工作要点, 请示, 报告, 方案.

Drafting checks:

- Do not make党委 directly perform administrative approval, licensing, punishment, procurement, or fiscal payment unless the document is about leadership/coordinating requirements.
- Use "牵头协调、督促落实、研究重大事项" for党委办公室、政法委、组织、宣传、统战、网信等党务机关 according to their portfolio.
- When a task affects行政执法、财政支出、项目审批、公共服务 delivery, assign implementation to政府及其部门 after党委提出方向 or统筹要求.

### 人大

Use人大 language for local legislation, statutory decisions, supervision, appointments/removals, budget/final accounts review, law-enforcement inspection, representative suggestions, and deliberation opinions.

Typical bodies and documents:

- 人民代表大会, 人大常委会, 主任会议, 专门委员会, 工作委员会.
- 决议, 决定, 审议意见, 执法检查报告, 议案, 代表建议办理答复, 任免文件.

Drafting checks:

- Do not write人大 as the administrative implementing unit for routine government work.
- For government-facing items, use "依法听取和审议专项工作报告、开展执法检查、组织专题询问、跟踪督办审议意见落实".
- For representative suggestions, identify主办单位、协办单位、办理时限、答复方式、满意度反馈、二次办理 or跟踪督办.

### 政府

Use政府 language for administrative management, public service, administrative decision-making, project implementation, fiscal budget execution, emergency response, market supervision, law enforcement, and departmental coordination.

Typical bodies and documents:

- 政府全体会议, 常务会议, 专题会议, 市长/区长办公会议, 部门办公会.
- 政府令, 决定, 通知, 通报, 请示, 报告, 批复, 函, 会议纪要, 工作方案.

Drafting checks:

- Use the latest local三定规定 and government工作规则 to allocate departmental duties.
- For重大行政决策, include public participation, expert consultation, risk assessment, legality review, collective discussion and decision, and post-evaluation when applicable.
- Distinguish政府办公室 coordination from部门履职. The office can coordinate,督办, and handle documents; the主管部门 should own professional implementation.

### 政协

Use政协 language for political consultation, democratic supervision, participation in and deliberation of state affairs, proposals, research, member work, and consensus building.

Typical bodies and documents:

- 政协全体会议, 常务委员会会议, 主席会议, 专题协商会, 提案委员会会议, 界别协商.
- 建议案, 提案, 调研报告, 社情民意信息, 协商纪要, 大会发言.

Drafting checks:

- Do not write政协 as issuing binding administrative orders to government departments.
- Use "围绕...开展协商议政、提出意见建议、跟踪提案办理、反映社情民意".
- For government interaction, phrase as "建议有关部门研究采纳" or "商请有关方面办理反馈", unless a local制度 provides a specific办理 mechanism.

## 三定方案 And 机构编制

"三定" generally means定职责、定机构、定编制. In drafts that assign duties or design organizations, treat the latest三定规定 and机构编制 approvals as controlling source material.

Before assigning responsibility:

- Confirm official department name,挂牌名称,内设机构,所属事业单位, and current改革后的职责.
- Check whether the matter is行政职能,公益服务,执法监管,行业管理,综合协调, or技术支撑.
- Identify主办单位, 牵头单位, 协办单位, 配合单位, 责任单位, and属地单位 with different verbs.
- Avoid assigning statutory enforcement or approval duties to临时机构、议事协调机构办公室,事业单位, or third-party bodies unless the legal basis is supplied.

Use verbs carefully:

- 主办/牵头: organize, prepare, coordinate, summarize, submit, report,督促.
- 协办/配合: provide data, participate in review, implement portfolio tasks, give professional opinions.
- 属地: implement territorial management, community work, site order, conflict mediation, emergency response support.
- 行业主管: policy guidance, project supervision, standard implementation, professional acceptance.
- 执法机关: investigate and handle violations within statutory authority.
- 财政部门: budget review, fund allocation according to procedures, performance management, fiscal supervision.
- 机构编制部门: review机构设置、职责调整、编制和领导职数 matters according to authority.

Red flags:

- Creating a new permanent office, center, team,牌子,领导小组办公室, or事业单位 without编制 approval.
- Assigning新增编制,领导职数,内设科室, or permanent posts in a work plan.
- Using "由某部门负责全部工作" when the task crosses legal, finance, emergency, publicity, petition, transport, and territorial portfolios.
- Naming outdated departments after reforms. Use "市/区有关主管部门" until verified.

## Meeting Flow And Agenda

For meetings, define the meeting's authority before drafting the agenda or minutes.

### Common meeting sequence

1. Topic initiation: sponsor submits issue description, basis, options, risk, budget, and proposed decision.
2. Pre-review: office checks procedure, materials, attendees, confidentiality, and whether legal/compliance/finance review is needed.
3. Consultation: seek opinions from affected departments, territories, experts, public representatives, or stakeholders when required.
4. Meeting approval: include topic in agenda after responsible leader or office review.
5. Meeting conduct: host, report, discussion, opinion collection, decision summary.
6. Minutes and decision list: clarify agreed matters, responsible units, deadlines, and reporting route.
7. Supervision: assign督办 unit, ledger, feedback cycle, and closure standard.
8. Archive: agenda, attendance, materials, opinions, minutes, sign-off, and implementation evidence.

### Agenda elements

Use this compact structure:

- Meeting name
- Time and place
- Presider
- Attendees, non-voting attendees, and invited units
- Recorder and confidentiality level if needed
- Topic list
- Reporting unit and reporter for each topic
- Materials submitted
- Issues for deliberation
- Proposed decision items
- Responsible units and completion deadline

### Meeting minutes checks

- Minutes should reflect decisions, not verbatim speeches unless required.
- Separate "会议听取了", "会议认为", "会议原则同意", "会议要求", "会议明确".
- Do not add matters not discussed or expand decisions beyond meeting authority.
- If a matter needs higher-level approval, write "按程序报..." rather than treating it as final.
- For implementation-heavy minutes, add a responsibility list or督办台账.

## 公务接待 And 政务接待

Use "公务接待" for official-duty visits, inspections, research, meetings, exchanges, and necessary reception within party/government/public institution work. Use "政务接待" only when local rules or event context use that term; otherwise prefer the formal category in the applicable local policy.

### Reception plan elements

A complete reception plan should include:

- Basis and purpose: official letter/invitation, approved task, visit reason, host unit.
- Visitors: unit, names, titles,人数,民族/外事/保密/健康 or accessibility needs when relevant.
- Itinerary: arrival/departure, routes, meeting, site visits, time buffer, weather alternative.
- Reception leadership: responsible leader, escorting officials, liaison officers, driver/vehicle contacts.
- Meeting agenda: host, speeches, reports, discussion topics, materials, seating, interpretation if needed.
- Site visit arrangement: point owner, route, briefing口径, safety, crowd control, photography rules.
- Accommodation and dining: standards,人数, location, menu control, special dietary needs, approval path.
- Transport: vehicle count, routes, parking, traffic support, emergency vehicle access.
- Security and emergency: on-site order, medical support, emergency contacts, public opinion response.
- Publicity: whether to report, press release reviewer, photo/video review, confidentiality boundaries.
- Budget and reimbursement: budget source, procurement or settlement path, invoices,接待清单, approval records.
- Discipline: no over-standard reception, gifts, tourism, unrelated visits, excessive陪同, or private expenses.

### Reception workflow

1. Receive official letter or task notice.
2. Verify reception necessity, level,人数, and funding source.
3. Draft plan and budget.
4. Submit for approval by host unit according to local rules.
5. Coordinate transport, meeting room, meals, accommodation, security, and publicity.
6. Execute with a liaison ledger and issue log.
7. Reconcile expenses and attach公函、审批单、接待清单、票据、菜单/行程 if required.
8. Archive and review problems.

### Common drafting fixes

- Replace "热情接待" with exact route, agenda, escort, room, materials, and expense control.
- Replace "做好服务保障" with vehicle, meal, accommodation, sign-in, materials, translation, security, medical, and public opinion tasks.
- If visitors include higher-level inspection teams, include整改反馈,资料清单,陪同边界,现场点位说明, and liaison responsibility.
- If reception involves enterprises, social groups, or foreign/Hong Kong/Macao/Taiwan guests, require additional compliance,外事,统战,商务, or confidentiality review as appropriate.

## Cross-Level Alignment

When the user asks to draw from central, provincial, municipal, and district public plans:

- Extract reusable structure, not unverifiable local facts.
- Preserve the user's jurisdiction and avoid copying another locality's department names unless the user asks for a comparative draft.
- Build a comparison table: topic, higher-level requirement, local responsible unit, required action, output, deadline, evidence.
- Include legality review, risk assessment, public participation, expert consultation, budget/performance, procurement, emergency response, and post-evaluation when the matter touches public interest or citizens' rights.
- Mark items needing local verification: latest三定规定, government工作规则, local meeting rules, reception standards, fiscal rules, emergency plan, and confidentiality classification.
