# Review Checklist

Use this reference for official document review, format checking, and language tightening.

## Authority And Purpose

- Confirm the sender has authority for the action.
- Confirm the document type matches the purpose.
- Confirm the main recipient relationship is correct.
- Confirm whether a legal, policy, meeting, or superior-document basis is needed.
- Confirm there is one clear main matter.
- Confirm the document does not mix 请示 and 报告.
- Confirm public-facing content has gone through disclosure and confidentiality review.

## Content Completeness

- Title accurately names the matter and document type.
- 发文字号, 签发人, 主送机关, 附件说明, 发文机关署名, 成文日期, 印章, 附注, 抄送机关, 印发机关, 印发日期, and 页码 are present when applicable.
- Background explains why the document is necessary.
- Requirements identify responsible units, deadlines, deliverables, and reporting path.
- Attachments are named consistently in body and attachment list.
- Contact person, phone, or office is included when implementation requires it.
- Date, issuing body, and seal/signature placeholders are present when needed.

## Format Quick Checks

- Use A4 paper and left-side binding conventions unless local template says otherwise.
- Use 3号仿宋体 for most elements unless a special element requires another style.
- Use 2号小标宋体 for the title where the standard template requires it.
- Use Chinese full-width punctuation in Chinese text.
- Use the six-angle brackets `〔〕` for document year in 发文字号, not `[]` or `()`.
- Use Arabic numerals for page numbers and statutory/date fields according to the template.
- Confirm attachments are listed after the body and match actual attached files.

## Language Standards

Prefer:
- "请结合实际认真贯彻落实"
- "现将有关事项通知如下"
- "经研究，现批复如下"
- "请于...前报送..."
- "由...负责，...配合"

Avoid:
- Casual wording: "大家抓紧弄一下", "尽快搞定"
- Empty intensity: "高度重视" without concrete action
- Ambiguous responsibility: "有关单位负责"
- Overbroad wording: "所有情况必须全部..."
- Unsupported claims: "取得显著成效" without evidence

## Common Red Flags

- A "报告" asks for approval.
- A "请示" contains multiple unrelated matters.
- A "通知" issues commands to bodies outside the sender's authority.
- A public notice exposes personal information unnecessarily.
- A workflow lacks sign-off authority or archive ownership.
- A document cites a policy without full name, number, or effective scope.
- Time limits are vague: "近期", "尽快", "适时".
- 发文字号 year uses the wrong brackets.
- 成文日期 differs from signing date without explanation or local rule.
- 附件说明 and actual attachment titles/numbers do not match.

## Review Output

Give a practical review:
- Overall verdict: usable, needs revision, or not recommended.
- Issues ordered by risk: authority/legal, facts, process, format, wording.
- Specific replacement wording.
- A revised draft when enough context exists.
- Questions only for facts that cannot be safely assumed.
