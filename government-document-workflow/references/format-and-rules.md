# Format And Current Rules

Use this reference before giving format, procedure, or compliance advice for mainland Chinese party/government official documents.

## Current Basis

As of 2026-06-11, use these as the baseline:

- 《党政机关公文处理工作条例》, issued by the General Office of the CPC Central Committee and the General Office of the State Council, effective 2012-07-01.
- GB/T 9704-2012《党政机关公文格式》, released 2012-06-29 and implemented 2012-07-01. The National Public Service Platform for Standards showed this standard as `现行` when checked on 2026-06-11.
- Do not use the former GB/T 9704-1999《国家行政机关公文格式》 as current; the standards platform showed it as `废止` when checked on 2026-06-11.

When precision matters, verify with the issuing body's latest local office rules and official templates.

## Official Document Elements

Common elements include:

- 份号
- 密级和保密期限
- 紧急程度
- 发文机关标志
- 发文字号
- 签发人
- 标题
- 主送机关
- 正文
- 附件说明
- 发文机关署名
- 成文日期
- 印章
- 附注
- 附件
- 抄送机关
- 印发机关和印发日期
- 页码

Typical grouping:

- 版头: elements above the red separator line.
- 主体: title through attachments/signature/date/seal/notes.
- 版记: copy recipients, printing office, printing date, and related footer elements.

## Page And Typography Baseline

Use local templates first. When none are provided, use the GB/T 9704-2012 baseline:

- Paper: A4.
- Binding: left binding.
- Body layout: generally 22 lines per page and 28 Chinese characters per line.
- Margins commonly applied from the standard template: top about 37 mm, bottom about 35 mm, left about 28 mm, right about 26 mm.
- General font: 3号仿宋体 unless the element has a specific requirement.
- Title: generally 2号小标宋体.
- First-level headings in body are often 黑体; second-level headings often 楷体; body text remains 仿宋体. Follow local style sheets where available.
- Page numbers: Arabic numerals, placed according to the standard template.

## Numbering And Dates

- 发文字号 generally follows `机关代字〔年份〕序号` and uses six-angle brackets `〔〕`.
- Do not use square brackets, parentheses, or a hyphenated year in 发文字号.
- 成文日期 should match the signing/approval rule used by the issuing body.
- Joint documents require special attention to signing order, date, and seal placement.

## Writing And Routing Rules

- 行文 must follow authority and organizational relationships.
- Generally do not send a 请示 to multiple superior authorities at the same time.
- Do not include requests for approval inside a 报告.
- Requests should normally be one matter per document.
- Departments may not issue command-like documents beyond their authority.
- Public release requires disclosure review; sensitive documents require confidentiality review.

## Handling Procedures

Public-sector workflow should preserve these procedural concepts:

- 公文拟制: 起草, 审核, 签发.
- 发文办理: 复核, 登记, 印制, 核发.
- 收文办理: 签收, 登记, 初审, 承办, 传阅, 催办, 答复.
- 整理归档: final version, attachments, approval chain, dispatch evidence, and handling result are filed according to archive rules.
